Our Disciplines.

A detailed look into our core capabilities, engineered for performance and durability.

We transform raw materials like steel, stainless steel, and aluminum into bespoke components and assemblies. From initial concept to final product, our fabrication process is defined by precision and a deep understanding of metal properties.

  • Prototyping & One-Off Parts
  • Structural Components & Frames
  • Platforms, Ladders & Handrails
  • Sheet Metal Work

Downtime is costly. We specialize in heavy-duty welding repairs for agricultural and industrial equipment, reinforcing weak points and replacing damaged sections to get your machinery back in service quickly and reliably.

  • Bucket & Attachment Repair
  • Frame & Chassis Reinforcement
  • Hardfacing for Wear Resistance
  • Trailer & Implement Repairs

Our machine shop is equipped with lathes, mills, and drills to create custom parts with exacting tolerances. Whether you need a replacement component or a newly designed part, we deliver precision you can count on.

  • Turning, Milling & Drilling
  • Shaft & Bushing Fabrication
  • Keyway Cutting
  • Custom Brackets & Mounts
